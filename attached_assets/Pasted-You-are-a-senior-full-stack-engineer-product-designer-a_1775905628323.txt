• Upload their CV

--------------------------------

AI CV ANALYSIS FEATURE

Job seekers can upload their CV.

The system sends the CV to OpenAI for analysis.

The AI should evaluate:

• Skills
• Experience
• CV formatting
• Job readiness
• Job match potential

The AI returns:

• CV improvement suggestions
• Skills breakdown
• Match score

--------------------------------

MONETIZATION

Users must pay $20 to unlock the AI CV review.

Payment flow:

Upload CV  
Prompt payment  
After payment unlock AI results

Users then choose:

1) Accept automated AI review  
2) Request human HR review

--------------------------------

EMPLOYER DASHBOARD

Employers should be able to:

• Post jobs
• Edit job listings
• View applicants
• Download CVs

--------------------------------

EMPLOYER AI FEATURE

Employers can upload multiple CVs.

The AI analyzes and compares CVs against a job description.

Return results:

• Candidate ranking
• Match score
• Skill comparison
